<br>

**Sam Rohan** holds unequivocal and perpetual title to all intellectual property constituting the essential identity and functional core of **ACI R&D**. This ownership is universal, inviolable, and extends to all derivatives, abstractions, and unique expressions thereof. It encompasses, without limitation:

1.  **Brand Identifiers:** The name "**ACI R&D**," its acronyms, all associated logos (2D and 3D representations), distinctive graphical assets, visual signatures, trade dress, and any textual or symbolic identifiers uniquely linked to the **ACI R&D** brand.
2.  **Operational Ethos & Foundational Frameworks:** The specific methodologies, governance models, and strategic frameworks defining **ACI R&D's** unique approach. 3.  **Core Programmatic Structures:** The architecture and defining principles of **ACI R&D's** global nexus model, its decentralized digital ecosystem design, its planetary collective intelligence framework, and its specific initiatives for proactive empowerment, collaborative vigilance, and ethical remediation as distinctively branded and operationalized by **ACI R&D**.
4.  **Proprietary Research & Development Outputs:** All research outputs, engineering designs (including open-hardware blueprints or PDKs released under **ACI R&D** branding), technological innovations (including specific decentralized governance models or AI frameworks), threat intelligence protocols, and training methodologies developed *internally* by, or exclusively *for*, **ACI R&D** and not explicitly released under standard FOSS licenses in its public repositories. This includes any unique syntheses or applications of broader concepts bearing the distinctive **ACI R&D** imprimatur or operational signature.

**Article 1: Zero Tolerance for Exploitation**
No entity—corporate, governmental, institutional, or individual—may reproduce, adapt, reverse-engineer, commercially exploit, or misappropriate the declared ACI R&D IP. Prohibited activities explicitly include:

- Filing trademark or copyright registrations, or asserting claims that imply co-ownership, affiliation, or endorsement by **ACI R&D** or **Sam Rohan** without explicit, written authorization.
- Engaging in brand impersonation, creating misleading affiliations, or parasitizing the reputation and goodwill inherent in the **ACI R&D** identity for any purpose.
- Incorporating **ACI R&D's** distinctive operational ethos, programmatic structures, or branded methodologies into competing or unrelated offerings.

**Article 2: Ban on Derivative Works and Rebranding**
Modification, rebranding, abstraction, or unauthorized derivation of **ACI R&D** logos, design elements, acronyms, core operational frameworks, or unique methodologies is strictly forbidden. The creation of derivative works—whether under alternate nomenclature, within ostensibly non-commercial projects, or under the guise of parody or critique—constitutes actionable infringement if it utilizes or evokes the protected essence of the **ACI R&D** identity or its proprietary operational core.

**Article 3: Limited Non-Commercial Exception**
Reference to **ACI R&D** or its foundational principles within academic discourse, journalistic reporting, or bona fide public security discussions is permitted solely under these stringent conditions:

1.  **Explicit Attribution:** The following mandatory statement must appear conspicuously adjacent to the reference: *"ACI R&D, its name, logos, and core operational frameworks are the exclusive intellectual property of Sam Rohan. Used strictly for informational purposes without endorsement or affiliation."*
2.  **Hyperlink Requirement:** All digital references must include a direct, functioning hyperlink to this official declaration.
3.  **Contextual Integrity:** References must accurately represent **ACI R&D's** mission and principles without distortion, misrepresentation, or implication of partnership. They cannot be used to promote other entities, products, or services.

<br>

**SECTION III: CLARITY ON DUAL NATURE – OPEN SOURCE VS. PROPRIETARY ASSETS**

To ensure absolute clarity regarding **ACI R&D’s** dual nature, this declaration definitively separates the domain of open-source collaboration from the domain of sovereign intellectual property.

1.  **ACI R&D's FOSS Initiatives:** **ACI R&D** actively publishes peer-reviewed research, tutorials, codebases, hardware documentation, and tools under standard open-source licenses (e.g., GPL, MIT, Apache) within its curated public repositories. These constitute its *democratized innovation* contributions and are governed by the terms of their respective licenses.
2.  **Scope of "Democratized Innovation":** The principle of democratizing innovation applies *solely* to assets explicitly released by **ACI R&D** under recognized FOSS licenses. It does not extend to, license, or diminish **Sam Rohan's** exclusive ownership of the **ACI R&D** identity, brand, core operational ethos, or internally developed proprietary frameworks as defined in Section I.
3.  **Participation Does Not Confer Ownership:** Participation in **ACI R&D's** ethical endeavors, open-source projects, or collaborative communities does not confer any rights to the underlying ACI R&D proprietary identity or methodologies. The "Cybersecurity Good Samaritan" ideal is a guiding principle, not a license for IP appropriation.

There is no contradiction, only a clear jurisdictional delineation.

<br>

**SECTION IV: ENFORCEMENT MECHANISMS**

Violations of this declaration will trigger immediate and comprehensive legal recourse across all applicable jurisdictions. Enforcement leverages international intellectual property statutes and related laws, including but not limited to:

- **Global Jurisdiction:** Legal actions will be initiated in all territories where **Sam Rohan's** rights are recognized or where infringement occurs, irrespective of the violator’s domicile.
